Emoni is a 21 year old student artist based in Northern California, Sacramento area. All of her artistic training has been in traditional realism, but recently, she has started to explore contemporary styles in digital form.

Emoni has been creating art and feeding various forms of creative expression since she was a child. Visual art has been her avenue of choice for the past five years, and she continues to seek out new knowledge surrounding her craft.

Ever learning, and ever evolving, the progression of her artistic style continuously offers bold and bright visuals, whilst offering subtle narrative.
